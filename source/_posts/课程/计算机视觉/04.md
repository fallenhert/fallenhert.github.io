## Line detection

change of variables $(x,y) \rightarrow (m,c)$

>$$
>\begin{aligned}
>&y = mx + c \\
>&m = \frac{y-c}{x} \\
>&m = -\frac{1}{x}c + \frac{y}{x} \\
>&m = Gc + D, where \ G = -\frac{1}{x},D = \frac{y}{x}
>\end{aligned}
>$$



change of variables $(x,y) \rightarrow (r,\theta)$

>$$
>\begin{aligned}
>& y = mx + c \\
>& m = -\frac{cos(\theta)}{sin(\theta)}, c = \frac{r}{sin(\theta)} \\
>&hence \ y_{i} = (-\frac{cos(\theta)}{sin(\theta)})x_{i} + (\frac{r}{sin(\theta)}) \\
>&so \ r = sin(\theta)y_{i} + cos(\theta)x_{i}
>\end{aligned}
>$$
>
>



## Circle detection

>$$
>\begin{aligned}
>& x_{c} = x_{i} - rcos(\theta_{i}) \\
>& y_{c} = y_{i} - rsin(\theta_{i})
>\end{aligned}
>$$
